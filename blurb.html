Complex Projects

Complex Projects is an art fabrication consultancy providing services to the creative industries.

Whether are looking to fully outsource the production of a sculpture or issue a set of laser patterns or shop drawings we can provide a tailored approach to your particular project and needs.

We are a flexible and dynamic young company looking for ambitious and technically challenging projects to get our teeth into. We are happy to be approached at any stage of a project and aim to provide advice, support and a web of contacts as much as we do to deliver completed pieces of work.
